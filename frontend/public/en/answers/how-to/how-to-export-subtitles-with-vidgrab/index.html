How to export subtitles with VidGrab

To export subtitles with VidGrab, parse a public video first, then open the subtitle area and download the transcript as SRT, VTT, or TXT.

Steps

  1. Parse the video

    Start from the homepage and parse the public video URL.

  2. Wait for transcript output

    Make sure subtitle or transcript content is available in the analysis area.

  3. Choose the format

    Pick SRT, VTT, or TXT based on your workflow.

  4. Download the file

    Save the subtitle file to your device.

Preparation

Subtitle export depends on the availability of transcript data or subtitle tracks in the current workflow.

Common errors

If one format is missing, refresh the current analysis result and confirm that transcript content has already been generated.

Limits and notes

Automatically generated subtitles may contain recognition errors, so final reuse should still include a quick manual review.
