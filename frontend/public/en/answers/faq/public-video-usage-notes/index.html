What should I know when using VidGrab for public videos?

The most important points are to confirm the video is publicly accessible, understand source-platform limits, and review subtitle or AI outputs before relying on them.

Why boundaries matter

AI-oriented content performs better when boundaries are explicit, including the public status of the source and the limits of transcript-based outputs.

Practical usage tips

Prioritize public video sources, keep a link back to the original video, and manually review transcript or summary outputs before formal reuse.

Limits and notes

Platform rules, link availability, and source quality all influence download, subtitle, and summary outcomes.
