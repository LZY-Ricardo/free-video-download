The main difference is that VidGrab adds subtitle export, AI summaries, mind maps, and follow-up Q&A on top of standard public-video downloading.

Regular downloaders mostly focus on saving files, while VidGrab is built around downloading, understanding, and organizing video content.

Who should choose VidGrab

Choose VidGrab when you need more than a file save, especially when the end goal is note-taking or AI-assisted analysis.

Limits and notes

If all you need is the fastest possible one-off file save, a basic downloader may be enough.
