Can VidGrab download subtitles?

Yes. VidGrab can export subtitles or transcript text from public videos as SRT, VTT, and TXT for reading, editing, and reuse.

Why subtitle export matters

Subtitles are easier to search, quote, translate, and save than long raw videos.

Common export formats

SRT works well for players and editing tools, VTT for web playback, and TXT for quick reading and AI processing.

Limits and notes

Subtitle availability still depends on whether the source video exposes subtitle tracks or transcript output.
